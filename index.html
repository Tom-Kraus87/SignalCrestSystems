About

Technology that keeps small businesses moving forward

I'm Tom Kraus, founder of SignalCrest Systems — a technology consultant serving small businesses across Greater Metro Detroit. I help organizations modernize their websites, stabilize their IT environments, and build digital systems that are secure, dependable, and ready to scale.

With 14 years across IT support, QA engineering, and systems administration, I take a hands-on, end-to-end approach: website design and development, cybersecurity, cloud and infrastructure, test automation, and the day-to-day technical support that keeps operations running. I've supported 500+ clients at SpotOn, built QA automation with Cypress.js in Agile teams, managed enterprise endpoints with SCCM and Intune, and resolved critical on-site escalations in high-pressure production environments — so I understand what small businesses actually need to stay secure and competitive.
